About Eagles World Wide

Eagles World Wide is a premier distributor of high-quality granite, marble, and other stone products for residential and commercial applications in Kenya and East Africa. With over 20 years of experience in the stone industry, we pride ourselves on our craftsmanship, attention to detail, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our extensive selection of premium stones sourced from around the world ensures that we can meet any design vision and budget. From kitchen countertops and bathroom vanities to fireplace surrounds and outdoor features, we offer comprehensive stone solutions that add lasting value and beauty to your space.

Our team of skilled artisans and designers works closely with each client to bring their vision to life, providing expert guidance throughout the entire process - from material selection to installation and beyond.
